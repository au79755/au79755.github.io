My doctoral thesis presents comprehensive research on automating AI/ML pipeline infrastructure, addressing the critical need for large-scale, diverse data in AI research and machine learning applications. The work demonstrates how advanced automation can transform traditional data collection bottlenecks into scalable, reproducible training pipelines - exemplifying the full-stack approach to AI development and deployment.
