Developed and showcased an enterprise-grade cloud platform that enabled remote control of multi-robot systems and automated data collection workflows across international locations. The system's intuitive web interface allowed researchers to orchestrate complex experiments, generating gigabytes of multimodal sensor data while providing real-time monitoring and digital twin visualization capabilities - demonstrating a 6x improvement in research data collection efficiency.
