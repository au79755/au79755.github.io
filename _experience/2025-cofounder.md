As the co-founder and CTO, I lead the technical vision and AI research for **PermitSight.** Our mission is to deploy sophisticated agentic systems that modernize the administrative backbone of American cities, turning complex regulatory data into actionable municipal intelligence.

### **Highlights**

* **Technical Strategy:** Defined our AI roadmap, streamlining adoption of cutting-edge developments in agentic AI, multimodal AI, reasoning, and multi-agent teams.
* **Research and Development:** Leveraged insights from academia and practitioners to productize our novel computer vision algorithm for engineering diagram comprehension.
* **Execution:** Deployed and delivered live tech demos of our software solution for various municipalities and at industry conferences across the USA.
