As the co-founder and CTO, I lead the company's technical vision, product architecture, and engineering execution. Our mission is to build a sophisticated AI platform to solve high-stakes challenges for enterprise clients.

### **Key Responsibilities**

* **Technical Strategy:** Defining the long-term technical roadmap, selecting technology stacks, and ensuring our architecture is scalable, secure, and aligned with business goals.
* **Product Architecture:** Designing the end-to-end system for our agentic AI platform, from data ingestion and processing pipelines to the user-facing application layer.
* **Team Leadership:** Building and managing the engineering team, fostering a culture of innovation and execution, and mentoring team members.
* **Execution:** Leading the hands-on development of core product features, including implementing functionalities for agentic orchestration.
