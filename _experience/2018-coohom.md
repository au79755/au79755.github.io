As an intern at Coohom, a high-growth SaaS platform for interior design, I was integrated into the development team to contribute to both frontend and backend tasks. This role provided foundational experience in a fast-paced, agile startup environment.

### **Key Responsibilities & Learnings**

* **Full-Stack Contribution:** Supported UI/UX and backend teams on various development tasks, gaining practical experience in the full software development lifecycle.
* **Automation & Localization:** Developed automation scripts to assist with software localization and testing.
* **Technical Growth:** Gained hands-on experience with modern web technologies, version control, and software testing methodologies in a startup setting.
