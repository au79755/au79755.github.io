I contributed to the Singer Research Group as a research assistant, developing algorithms and full-stack systems for multimodal AI and robotics while authoring several publications.

### **Highlights**

* **Multimodal AI:** Designed algorithms for high-dimensional image and audio processing, including segmentation and enhancement tasks.
* **IoT & Sensing:** Optimized on-device sensor processing and data pipelines to support real-time control and enhancement algorithms.
* **Multi-Robot Platforms:** Built full-stack software for robotic research platforms with real-time control, multimodal sensing, and web-based orchestration.
* **Outreach:** Delivered tech demos of our remote research platform to audiences across Illinois, Tennessee, New York, and Germany.
